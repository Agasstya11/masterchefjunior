pesto pasta
-spagetti
-nuts(pine)
-basil leaves
salt and peper
other spices(optional)
-cream(optional)
-cheese(optional)
-EVOO
-vegetables(optional)
boil the pasta for a few mins, to chec if the pasta is cooked try a piece, after that take a mixi, and add basil leaves, pine nuts,salt and pepper, cream, cheese, extra virgin olive oil and then combine them.
 when everthing is ready, take a pan, add the assortment of veggies, the pasta and the pesto sauce, cook it under high for a min and then serve it with cheese.I hope you liked this easy but tasty recipe for my favourite dish, pesto pasta!
